### From the Heat of the Kitchen to the Heat of an Incident

For the last 8 years, I've been a Lead Line Cook at **Via Italian Table**. People often ask me why I’m moving into **Cybersecurity**. On the surface, they seem like opposite worlds, but they both rely on one thing: **Mise en Place.**

In the kitchen, if your station isn't set up, you fail the rush. In a Security Operations Center (SOC), if your tools aren't configured and your logs aren't monitored, you miss the breach.

### What I’m Working On
I'm currently building out my skills to transition into a SOC Analyst or Junior Pentester role:
* **Home SOC Lab:** Setting up a ThinkCentre with **Wazuh** to monitor my network.
* **Mobile Pentesting:** Playing with my **Pwnagotchi** (Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W) to learn about Wi-Fi handshakes.
* **Certifications:** Currently deep in the **Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate** and prepping for **Security+**.

Stay tuned as I document my journey from Worcester's busiest kitchens to the front lines of digital defense.
